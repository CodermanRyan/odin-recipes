An eggplant pasta dish

The eggplant pasta dish is infused with olive oil, garlic, tomatoes and is delicious

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 eggplant, peeled and cut into 1.3 centimeter cubes
  • 1 can plum tomatoes with juice, chopped
  • 1 package rigatoni pasta

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil. Cook and stir garlic in for 2 mins. Add eggplant for 5 mins. Add tomato paste for 20 mins.
  2. Cook pasta in boiling water, while stirring for about 13 minutes.
  3. Pour sauce over pasta.
