Mexican Pasta

A Mexican pasta dish

The Mexican pasta dish is infused with olive oil, onions, green pepper and is tasty

Ingredients

  • 227 grams seashell pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • Half a cup sweet corn kernels
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 can peeled and diced tomatoes
  • Half a cup salsa
  • A quarter cup sliced black olives
  • 1.5 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil large slightly salted pot. Add pasta and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. While pasta is cooking, heat olive oil over medium heat in a large skillet. Cook onions and bell pepper in oil until lightly browned, about 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in corn and heat through. Stir in black beans, tomatoes, salsa, olives, seasoning mix, and salt and pepper and cook until thoroughly heated, about 5 minutes.
  4. Toss sauce with cooked pasta and serve.
