Roasted Veggie Pasta

A roasted veggie pasta dish

The roasted veggie pasta dish is filled with veggies and earth's goodness

Ingredients

  • 113 grams fresh asparagus
  • 2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 113 grams crimini mushrooms, sliced
  • 10 cloves roasted garlic, chopped
  • Half a tomato, quartered
  • Half a teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • Half a teaspoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 226 grams dry fettuccini noodles
  • A quarter cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons tapenade

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C. Prepare asparagus by trimming woody base and cutting diagonally into 4 2.54 centimeter pieces.
  2. In a roasting pan, combine asparagus, bell pepper, mushrooms, roasted garlic and tomato. Sprinkle with rosemary and oregano, then drizzle with olive oil. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente; drain. Toss with Parmesan cheese, tapenade and roasted vegetables.
